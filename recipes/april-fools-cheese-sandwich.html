April Fool's Grilled Cheese Sandwich

The final result!

Easy recipe to joke with your friends on April Fool's Day, watch out for revenges!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound cake, cut into 1/2-inch slices
  • 6 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 1 cup white frosting
  • ½ teaspoon yellow food coloring, or as needed
  • ⅛ teaspoon red food coloring, or as needed

Directions

  1. Step 1:Spread butter on both sides of each pound cake slice.
  2. Step 2:Heat a griddle over medium heat. Cook cakes slices on hot griddle, turning once, until golden brown on each side, 3 to 5 minutes. Transfer cake slices to a wire rack to cool completely.
  3. Step 3:Stir frosting, yellow food coloring, and red food coloring together in a bowl until frosting is the color of American cheese.
  4. Step 4:Spread frosting on 1 side of half of the pound cake slices and top with another slice of pound cake.
